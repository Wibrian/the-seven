The Seven Archon of Teyvat

Typically shortened to The Seven, are the seven gods who preside over the seven regions ofTeyvat, established after seven gods emerged as the victors of the Archon War 2,000 years ago.

  • Barbatos
  • Morax
  • Beelzebul
  • Buer
  • Focalor
